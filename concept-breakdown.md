# SAT Math Concept Breakdown - Geometry and Trigonometry

## Skill Tree Structure

### 1. LINES & ANGLES (Foundation)
**Sub-concepts:**
- 1.1 Angle Relationships
  - Vertical angles (equal)
  - Supplementary angles (sum to 180°)
  - Complementary angles (sum to 90°)

- 1.2 Parallel Lines & Transversals
  - Corresponding angles (equal when parallel)
  - Alternate interior angles (equal when parallel)
  - Same-side interior angles (supplementary when parallel)

- 1.3 Linear Pairs
  - Adjacent angles on a straight line (sum to 180°)

### 2. TRIANGLES (Core)
**Sub-concepts:**
- 2.1 Triangle Angle Sum
  - All interior angles sum to 180°
  - Finding missing angles

- 2.2 Congruent Triangles
  - Corresponding angles are equal
  - Corresponding sides are equal

- 2.3 Similar Triangles
  - Corresponding angles are equal
  - Corresponding sides are proportional
  - Scale factors

- 2.4 Isosceles Triangles
  - Two equal sides
  - Base angles are equal

- 2.5 Triangle Perimeter
  - Sum of all three sides

### 3. RIGHT TRIANGLES (Intermediate)
**Sub-concepts:**
- 3.1 Pythagorean Theorem
  - a² + b² = c²
  - Finding hypotenuse
  - Finding legs

- 3.2 Trigonometric Ratios
  - Sine (opposite/hypotenuse)
  - Cosine (adjacent/hypotenuse)
  - Tangent (opposite/adjacent)

- 3.3 Similar Right Triangles
  - Proportional relationships
  - Shadow problems

### 4. QUADRILATERALS (Core)
**Sub-concepts:**
- 4.1 Rectangle Properties
  - Opposite sides equal
  - All angles are 90°

- 4.2 Rectangle Perimeter
  - P = 2(length + width)

- 4.3 Rectangle Area
  - A = length × width

- 4.4 Square Properties
  - All sides equal
  - All angles are 90°

- 4.5 Square Area
  - A = side²

### 5. 3D GEOMETRY (Intermediate)
**Sub-concepts:**
- 5.1 Rectangular Prism Volume

- 5.2 Working with Units
  - Cubic units for volume
